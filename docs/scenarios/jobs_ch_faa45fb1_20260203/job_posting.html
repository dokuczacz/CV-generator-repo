About the job

Following a series of exciting acquisitions, Dec Group is accelerating its growth and broadening its product portfolio — particularly in aseptic and containment filling lines and isolator technologies.

 

As a global leader in the design and production of powder transfer, handling, and containment equipment for the pharmaceutical and chemical industries, Dec Group provides end-to-end solutions across the entire production chain — from raw material handling to final product packaging.

With more than 950 employees worldwide, including 120 team members at our Swiss headquarters, we continue to expand and innovate to meet the challenges of tomorrow.

To support our ongoing growth, we are looking to connect with passionate professionals who are ready to make an impact.

Project Manager Engineering (M/F) – 100 %

As a key member of our project management team, you will lead and oversee complex process engineering projects — ensuring smooth execution from design to delivery.

 

You will:

  • Take ownership of multiple process projects across all phases (Design, Manufacturing, FAT, SAT)
  • Develop detailed process documentation (P&ID, Functional Specifications)
  • Define project timelines and track key deliverables
  • Manage project budgets and ensure cost efficiency
  • Act as the main point of contact for clients and ensure seamless cross-functional collaboration internally

 

Your profile

  • Degree in Engineering (process, mechanical, or equivalent)
  • Proven experience in process engineering or project management, ideally in a similar environment
  • Fluent in English; German is a strong asset
  • Proactive, dynamic, and adaptable personality with strong initiative
  • Excellent organizational and problem-solving skills, able to perform under pressure
  • Strong communication skills and a genuine team player mindset

 

Why join us?

  • Join a fast-growing international company with a leading reputation in life sciences engineering
  • Take on a visible and impactful role with real responsibility
  • Work in a collaborative, innovative environment where your ideas are valued
  • Contribute to projects shaping the future of biotech and pharma production
  • Be part of a team that combines technical excellence with a passion for innovation
